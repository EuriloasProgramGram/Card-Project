1804's
REALISM
  • Realism was the most popular among the painter's world of art back then.
  • Gustave Courbet was the first to do the realism movement of art.
  • Realism was known by a subjects painted every day of lives like walking, relaxing, and others in a naturalistic manner.
  • Popular painters like Da Vinci and Van Gogh used realism to paint their art and their art was still there in the art museum to this day.
1910's
ABSTRACT ART
  • Abstract art was known to not attempting an accurate depiction by using shapes, colors, and others.
  • Abstract art was known beautiful not because it is, it's from the open mind and interpretation of it with those who open their minds.
  • The 6 elements of abstract art is Line, Texture, Shape, Form, Color, and Value.
  • The reason why it got so popular was because artists could now delve deeper into themselves and create art that was not just depicting things and objects as everyone saw them.

  • Pop art was known for in Britain and the United States, and they used popular cultures into imagery.
  • The most famous piece was the Campbell's soup cans in different canvases with a different flavor.
  • The most famous person in pop art was Andy Warhol, understanding the 'star-culture' ads, and many more using these in his work.
  • Comic books do use pop art to give out dynamic and even "pop" in the books.
19th century
MODERN ART
  • Picasso was the most influences person of modern art due to the shapes and color he uses and how unique it is.
  • Modern art was known for a philosophical feel and give out the style from the 1960's to the 1970's.
  • Modern art had faced heavy criticism due to departing the traditional matter, exploring new themes, and even believed that it had a lacked of skill.
  • Édouard Manet is the first modern artist, he inspired many young artists in the era.
1960's and 1970's
MINIMALISM
  • It was known to be an extreme abstract art style using simple shapes, hard edges, and exposing the essence of the forms and material that was use.
  • The reason why Minimalism was made was to show an attempt to explore the essential elements of an art form.
  • Frank Stella was the first minimalism artist, back in the late 1950's he'd made his famous black stripe paintings.
  • The feeling of minimalism art gave serenity and curiosity for the people who saw the pieces.
1938's
OPTICAL ART
  • Optical art was made to deceive the human mind making the art move of warping and swelling, or even jiggling.
  • Optical art was known as optical illusions and most art uses black in white in their paintings.
  • Optical art uses patterns, colors, and even shapes to make them blur or even move a certain way.
1952's
ACTION PAINTING
  • Action painting is like a child trying to paint but failed so, they got frustrated and pour paint everywhere, this is what action painting is, it shows vigorous strokes,spills, even splotches.
  • Action painting represent human emotions, it shows how mix up emotions are, of anger, sadness, happiness, and emotions are complex.
  • Jackson Pollock was famous for his action painting, he was leading an art movement of Abstract Expressionism which is an art movement characterized by the free-associative gestures in paint but famously known as action painting.
